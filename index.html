Interesting Facts about the Pigeon

  • Pigeons are very smart, and they can also recognise each letter of the human alphabet, differentiate between photographs, and even distinguish different humans within a photograph.
  • Pigeons are very social animals. They will often be seen in flocks of 20-30 birds.
  • Pigeons use a range of skills to navigate, such as using the sun as a guide and an internal magnetic compass of some kind to find their way around.

Interesting Facts about the Panda

  • Baby pands learn to climb trees at 5 months.
  • Giant pandas spend 10-16 hours a day eating, mainly on bamboo.
  • Sometimes, to mark their scent, panda's climb a tree backwards with their hindfeet until they're in a full handstand upside down - enabling them to leave their scent higher up.

Interesting Facts about the Cicada

  • Cicadas live on all continents except Antarctica.
  • They have one of the longest insect life spans.
  • They only emerge when the ground is 64 degrees.
